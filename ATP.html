How to understand the draws?

ATP##: The number after the ATP is the number of points awarded to the winner

In the draws next to the player's name you can find number or initials.

    The most common are:
  • #: Lists the seeded players
  • WC: Wild Card, enter the tournament by an invitation
  • PR: Protected Ranking, players' rankings before a prolonged injury
  • LL: Lucky Looser, player that lost during qualifying session but entered the main draw anyway
